# Eldervale — Bestiary

**Type:** Monster
**Tags:** bestiary, monsters, enemies, combat, eldervale

Each entry lists rough combat values for the rule engine: HP, Armor Class (AC), and attack bonus (ATK). The GM scales encounters to the party.

## Draugr (Barrow-Walker)
The corporeal undead of Eldervale — bloated, blackened, reeking of the grave, and far stronger than the living. They guard howes and the treasures within, retaining cunning and old grudges. They are slain only by decapitation and fire; a wound that would fell a man only angers them.
**HP:** 18 | **AC:** 13 | **ATK:** +4 | **Trait:** half damage from non-fire weapons; immune to fear.

## Haugbui (Howe-Lord)
A greater draugr bound to a single burial mound, obsessively guarding its grave-goods. Can grow huge, shape-shift to seal, bull, or horse, and curse those who rob its hoard. Bargaining (leaving a grave-good) may pass it without a fight.
**HP:** 34 | **AC:** 15 | **ATK:** +6 | **Trait:** curse on a hit (disadvantage next turn); placated by a freely-given grave-gift.

## Sky-Wolf Whelp (Spawn of Sköll and Hati)
Since the Shatter, gaunt grey wolves with starlight in their eyes hunt the fells in packs, drawn to the Shards. The whelps are the lesser kin of the wolves prophesied to devour sun and moon.
**HP:** 10 | **AC:** 14 | **ATK:** +3 | **Trait:** pack tactics (+2 ATK when an ally is adjacent to the target).

## Grove-Wight
A twisted guardian-spirit of a burned sacred grove, neither truly alive nor dead, lashing out at any who carry fire or wear the cross. Calmed by an Order rite (Mirra, Religion).
**HP:** 16 | **AC:** 12 | **ATK:** +3 | **Trait:** enraged by fire (+2 ATK vs those who used fire this scene); soothed by galdr.

## Iron Compact Reaver
Not a monster but a man — a sworn brother of the Iron Compact, disciplined, armored, and oath-bound never to flee. Fights in a shield-line and respects a fair surrender.
**HP:** 22 | **AC:** 16 | **ATK:** +5 | **Trait:** holds the line (allies in formation gain +1 AC); will accept yield.

## Secrets
- [GM ONLY] A draugr that was, in life, someone a party member knew will speak their name — and a player who answers it truthfully can lay it to rest without a fight (an Insight/Religion check, Mirra's specialty), turning a combat into a grief.
- [GM ONLY] The Sky-Wolf Whelps grow bolder the longer the Wound stays open; if the party dawdles, scale packs from 2 to 5 and add the trait "starving" (+1 ATK). This is the campaign's invisible clock.
- [GM ONLY] Astrid Hel-tongue can command all draugr and haugbui; if the party makes a court_pact with her, these enemies stand down across the realm.
