# Eldervale — Items and Artifacts

**Type:** Item
**Tags:** items, artifacts, treasure, shards, eldervale

## The Shards of Máni
Fragments of the broken moon, fallen across Eldervale as cold moon-stone that hums faintly at night and brighter near the Wound. Each holds a sliver of the power that once held the sky whole. Gathered at the Well of Urðr they can re-seal the night — or, mispoken, tear it wider. They are the object of every faction's hunt.

**Known facts:** Cold to the touch, never warm. Glow stronger under the Wound. Draugr are drawn to them. No mortal smith can work them.

## Moon-Ash
Pale dust that drifts down from the Wound on still nights. Rubbed into old carvings it makes pre-Shatter runes legible. The Order of the Silver Root collects it; the Hollow Court forbids its gathering.

**Known facts:** Reveals hidden runes (e.g., the Ruined Chapel's eye-sigil). Faintly luminous. Gathered in horn vials.

## The Notched Shield of Ironvale
Bran's shield, hacked and re-bound a dozen times, each notch a battle survived. Not magical — but it has never broken, and the party has learned to trust the line it holds.

**Known facts:** Bran's signature gear. Grants steadiness, not sorcery.

## Seiðstafr — the Völva's Staff
An iron-bound distaff carried by elders of the Order of the Silver Root, used to focus seiðr and walk the threads of fate. Since the Shatter the staves spark and bite; Lyra carries a lesser one.

**Known facts:** Focuses seiðr and divination. Dangerous to use since the Shatter. Marks an Order elder.

## Hacksilver and Oath-Rings
The coin of Eldervale: cut silver weighed on the spot, and arm-rings sworn over for oaths and alliances. An oath-ring given and later broken is the deepest insult in the north.

**Known facts:** Silver weighed, not counted. Oath-rings bind alliances. Breaking an oath-ring invites blood-feud.

## Grave-Goods of the Howes
Weapons, rings, and treasures buried with the honored dead for their journey to the afterlife. Since the Shatter, draugr guard them jealously, and taking them without leaving an exchange invites a haugbui's wrath.

**Known facts:** Buried with the dead. Guarded by draugr. Taking one may demand a gift in return.

## Secrets
- [GM ONLY] A whole (uncracked) Shard sits in the Memory Vault; all others in Eldervale are cracked fragments. Only the whole Shard can complete the re-sealing — the cracked ones merely point toward the Well.
- [GM ONLY] Moon-ash gathered directly from the Wound (not fallen ash) is lethally cold and will kill an unwarded gatherer; the Order knows the warding galdr, the Hollow Court wants the secret.
- [GM ONLY] An oath-ring sworn to Kael Thorn and kept is what moves his trust_level upward; one broken drops it to hostile permanently.
